## Banish Water Spots: Your Guide to Sparkling Glass

Do you see cloudy marks on your shower doors, windows, or car? These are water spots, and they often cause frustration. Water spots ruin the clear look of glass surfaces. They build up over time from mineral deposits in tap water. We all want crystal-clear glass. Knowing how to **clean water spots off glass** correctly makes a big difference. This guide will show you simple, effective methods to restore your glass. You will learn about different cleaning agents and prevention tips. Say goodbye to cloudy glass and hello to shine.
