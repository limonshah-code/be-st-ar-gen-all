## Getting Started: Preparing Your New Balance 2002r for Cleaning

Before you begin the cleaning process, proper preparation is important. This step helps prevent dirt from spreading and makes the cleaning more effective. Taking a few moments here saves time later on. I always start by getting everything ready.

You need to remove the laces and insoles from your shoes. Laces collect a lot of dirt, and insoles can hold odor. Cleaning them separately ensures a deeper clean for the whole shoe. This removes loose mud, dust, and debris. Skipping this step can turn dirt into mud once water is introduced. Make sure you brush in one direction to lift dirt away.

Consider the materials of your New Balance 2002r. Most pairs feature a mix of delicate suede and breathable mesh. Each material needs a specific cleaning approach. ## Deep Cleaning the Uppers: Suede and Mesh Techniques

The upper part of your New Balance 2002r is where dirt often shows most. These shoes blend suede and mesh, each needing a gentle, distinct cleaning method. You cannot use the same cleaner on both materials. I always separate my approach for these areas. This ensures effective cleaning without harm.

### Cleaning New Balance 2002r Suede

Suede is a delicate material. It requires a specific cleaning process to avoid damage or water stains. You must avoid saturating suede with water. This can cause discoloration or stiffening. Many products exist specifically for suede care.

Start with a dry suede brush. Gently brush the dirty areas in one direction to lift surface dirt and revive the nap. If you have scuffs or stubborn marks, a suede eraser can help. Rub the eraser gently over the mark. This action lifts the dirt. For deeper stains, use a specialized suede cleaner. Apply a small amount of cleaner to a clean cloth or a soft brush. Gently dab or lightly scrub the stained area. Do not rub too hard. Afterwards, wipe with a damp, clean cloth. Be very careful with the amount of moisture you use. For more detailed instructions on this delicate material, refer to our guide on [how to clean New Balance suede shoes](https://beacleaner.com/how-to-clean-new-balance-suede-shoes). This will help you keep your suede soft and vibrant.

### Cleaning New Balance 2002r Mesh

Mesh is more forgiving than suede but still needs gentle care. It can trap dust and dirt easily. This material usually cleans up well with a mild solution. You can see how this differs from methods for other shoes, like how to clean New Balance Fresh Foam shoes, which have different mesh types.

Create a mild cleaning solution. Mix a small amount of mild laundry detergent or shoe cleaner with warm water. Use a soft brush or a clean microfiber cloth. Dip the brush or cloth into the solution. Gently scrub the mesh in small circular motions. Do not press too hard. The goal is to lift dirt from the fibers, not push it deeper. Once the area is clean, wipe it with a separate clean, damp cloth. This removes any soap residue. Make sure you do not over-saturate the mesh. Excess water can affect the shoe's structure or take longer to dry. Simple dabbing motions are often best for final rinses.

## Tackling the Midsoles and Outsoles: Restoring the Foundation

The midsoles and outsoles of your New Balance 2002r endure the most contact with the ground. They collect significant dirt, scuffs, and grime. Cleaning these parts effectively restores the overall look of your shoes. This step is important for both aesthetics and hygiene. I always dedicate extra effort to these sections.

The midsole of the 2002r, often featuring ACTEVA LITE and N-ergy cushioning, can show dirt easily. For these areas, a simple cleaning solution works best. Mix warm water with a small amount of mild dish soap or shoe cleaner. Dip a medium-bristled brush, like an old toothbrush, into the solution. Scrub the midsole surfaces. Pay attention to any textured areas or crevices where dirt might hide. The goal is to gently lift and remove the dirt without damaging the foam.

For stubborn scuffs on the midsole, a magic eraser can be very effective. Lightly dampen the magic eraser. Gently rub it over the scuff marks. The abrasive nature of the eraser helps to lift the marks without heavy scrubbing. Be careful not to press too hard, as it can sometimes remove very thin layers of paint or finish if used too aggressively. After scrubbing, wipe the area with a clean, damp cloth to remove any residue.

The outsole, or bottom of your shoe, collects the most direct dirt. You can use the same soap solution and a stiffer brush for the outsole. A stiff-bristled brush or even a plastic scrub brush works well here. Vigorously scrub the rubber treads. This removes embedded dirt and small stones. Rinse the brush frequently in clean water to prevent spreading dirt. Once clean, wipe down the outsole with a damp cloth. This leaves the rubber fresh and ready for more wear. Regular cleaning of these areas helps maintain the shoe's appearance and grip. This makes your shoes look cared for from top to bottom.

## Addressing Stubborn Stains: Spot Treatment Strategies

Even with regular cleaning, stubborn stains can appear on your New Balance 2002r. These marks require targeted spot treatment. Different types of stains respond best to different approaches. Knowing how to deal with specific blemishes helps preserve your shoes. I keep a few specific tools ready for these situations.

For common dirt marks or light scuffs on non-suede areas, a simple mixture of mild soap and water often works. Apply the solution directly to the stain with a soft cloth or brush. Gently rub the area in small circles. Then, wipe away the soap and dirt with a clean, damp cloth. Repeat this process until the stain disappears. Avoid harsh chemicals, as they can damage the shoe materials or alter colors.

Oil or grease stains are more challenging. For these, a small amount of cornstarch or talcum powder can help. Sprinkle the powder generously over the oil stain. Let it sit for several hours, or even overnight. The powder absorbs the oil. Afterwards, gently brush off the powder. You may need to follow up with a mild soap solution. This method is especially effective on textile or mesh areas. Do not use this method on suede without consulting a specialized suede degreaser.

For mud or dried dirt, let the mud dry completely first. Then, gently tap the shoes together to dislodge large clumps. Use a stiff brush to carefully brush off the remaining dry dirt. This prevents mud from smearing into the fabric. After dry brushing, you can proceed with the standard cleaning methods for suede and mesh. Remember, patience is key when dealing with set-in stains. Multiple light applications are better than one aggressive attempt. Always test any new cleaning product on an inconspicuous area first. This ensures it does not cause discoloration or damage. This approach helps maintain the integrity and look of your 2002r shoes.

## Proper Drying: Essential for Protecting Your 2002r

After cleaning your New Balance 2002r, proper drying is a crucial step. Incorrect drying can lead to several problems. These include material shrinkage, color fading, or even odor development. You must allow your shoes to dry naturally and completely. I have learned that patience in this step pays off.

First, gently blot any excess water from the shoes with a clean towel. Press lightly to absorb moisture from the uppers, especially on mesh areas. Do not wring or twist the shoes. This can distort their shape. The goal is to remove surface water.

Next, reshape your shoes. Stuff the inside of each shoe with clean paper towels or newspaper. This helps absorb internal moisture and maintains the shoe's original form as it dries. Change the paper every few hours if it becomes too damp. This speeds up the drying process. Avoid using colored newspaper, as the ink can transfer to the shoe's interior.

Place your New Balance 2002r in a well-ventilated area. Choose a spot away from direct sunlight or artificial heat sources. Direct sunlight can fade colors and crack materials like rubber or leather. High heat from radiators or hair dryers can cause materials to shrink or warp. An airy room temperature environment is ideal. Allow them to air dry for at least 24 to 48 hours. The drying time can vary based on humidity and how wet the shoes got during cleaning. Ensure they are completely dry before you wear them again. Wearing damp shoes can promote bacterial growth and lead to unpleasant odors. This careful drying process helps maintain the shoe's comfort and appearance. It also prolongs its lifespan.

## Odor Removal and Freshness: Keeping Your Shoes Pleasant

Even after a thorough cleaning, shoes can sometimes retain a lingering odor. This is common, especially if you wear them often. Addressing shoe odor ensures your New Balance 2002r stays fresh and pleasant. I always take extra steps to keep my shoes smelling clean.

First, ensure your shoes are completely dry inside. Moisture is the primary cause of odor-causing bacteria. If your shoes are damp, allow them to air dry as described in the previous section. You can also place them near a fan to speed up the process. A dry environment discourages bacterial growth.

For effective odor absorption, baking soda is a powerful natural solution. Sprinkle a generous amount of baking soda inside each shoe. Make sure to cover the entire insole area. You can even pour some into the toe box. Let the baking soda sit overnight, or for at least 8 hours. It absorbs moisture and neutralizes odors. In the morning, simply shake out the excess baking soda. You can also vacuum the inside of the shoes to remove all residue. This leaves your shoes smelling much fresher.

Alternatively, you can use commercially available shoe deodorizers or odor-absorbing packets. These often contain activated charcoal or other materials designed to trap odors. Place them inside your shoes when you are not wearing them. Cedar shoe trees also work well. They absorb moisture and impart a fresh, natural scent. Maintaining a clean and dry internal environment is key to long-term freshness. ## Frequently Asked Questions About Cleaning New Balance 2002r

### Can I put my New Balance 2002r in the washing machine?
No, putting New Balance 2002r shoes in a washing machine is not recommended. The machine's harsh cycles can damage the delicate suede, mesh, and adhesive components. It can cause shrinkage, fading, or structural damage. Hand washing is always the safest method for these shoes.

### How do I clean the white parts of my New Balance 2002r?
For white midsoles, use a mild soap solution with a brush or a magic eraser for scuffs. For white mesh, use a gentle brush with mild soap and water. For white suede, use a specialized suede cleaner and brush, being careful not to over-saturate.

### What is the best way to remove mud from my 2002r?
Let the mud dry completely first. Once dry, gently tap the shoes together to remove large clumps. Use a stiff brush to brush off the remaining dry mud. Then, proceed with the standard cleaning methods for suede and mesh.

### How often should I clean my New Balance 2002r?
The frequency depends on how often you wear them and what conditions they encounter. For regular wear, a quick dry brush after each use is good. A deep clean every few weeks or months is advisable to maintain their appearance and prevent dirt buildup.

### Can I use a regular brush on the suede parts of my 2002r?
No, it is best to use a specialized suede brush. Regular brushes can be too harsh and may damage the suede's nap or cause discoloration. A suede brush is designed to lift dirt gently while restoring the material's texture.

### What if my 2002r shoes develop an odor after cleaning?
Ensure your shoes are completely dry inside. Then, sprinkle baking soda generously inside each shoe and leave it overnight to absorb odors. You can also use shoe deodorizers or cedar shoe trees to maintain freshness.
