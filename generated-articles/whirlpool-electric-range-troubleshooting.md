## Whirlpool Electric Range Troubleshooting: Fix Common Oven & Burner Issues

### **No Power or Partial Power Issues for Your Whirlpool Electric Range**

When your Whirlpool electric range shows no power, it is frustrating. This problem often starts outside the appliance. I always check the power supply first. A range needs a lot of power. It uses a 240-volt circuit. If only some parts work, like the clock but not the burners, it suggests a partial power loss. This means one leg of the 240-volt supply is missing.

#### **Basic Power Check**
