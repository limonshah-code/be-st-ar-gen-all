## Detailed Hand Washing Process for Hey Dudes

Now that your Hey Dudes are ready, it is time to wash them. This is the main cleaning step. Be gentle throughout the process. The goal is to clean without damaging the fabric. I always work slowly. This ensures good results.

Start by mixing your cleaning solution. In your small bowl, combine cool water and a small amount of mild soap. Use only a few drops of soap. Too much soap creates excessive suds. It also makes rinsing harder. Stir the water and soap until it is slightly bubbly. The water should feel slightly soapy to the touch. This simple mix is powerful enough.

Dip your soft-bristle brush into the solution. Do not saturate the brush. You want it damp, not soaking wet. Begin scrubbing the fabric of your Hey Dudes. Work on small sections at a time. Use gentle, circular motions. Focus on dirty areas. If your Hey Dudes are white, you might need extra care. White shoes show dirt easily. Check out our guide on [how to clean white Hey Dudes](https://beacleaner.com/how-to-clean-white-hey-dudes) for more tips. This method lifts dirt from the fibers. It does not push it deeper.

After scrubbing a section, wipe it with a clean, damp cloth. The cloth removes the dirty soap solution. Rinse the cloth often in clean water. Keep wiping until all soap residue is gone. Soap left on the shoes can attract more dirt. It can also leave marks. Repeat this process for all parts of the shoe. Pay attention to seams and edges. These areas often collect dirt. The sole also needs cleaning. Use the brush on the rubber parts. Make sure to clean all sides of the shoes.

For tougher spots, you might need to repeat scrubbing. Do not use strong force. Gentle, repeated action is better. This whole process takes time. But it gives the best results. Your Hey Dudes will look much cleaner. The fabric will feel fresh. This step-by-step method makes hand washing easy. It protects your shoes.

## Tackling Stubborn Stains on Your Hey Dudes

Sometimes, your Hey Dudes get more than just general dirt. They might have specific, stubborn stains. These need extra attention. You should treat stains before you do a full wash. This increases the chance of removing them completely. I have found this approach very effective.

First, identify the type of stain. Different stains need different approaches. For mud or dirt stains, let them dry completely. Once dry, you can easily brush off most of the dirt. For liquid stains like coffee or soda, blot them immediately. Do not rub the stain. Rubbing spreads it. It also pushes the liquid deeper into the fabric. Blotting lifts the liquid out.

For most common stains, a slightly stronger soap solution helps. You can make a paste with baking soda and a little water. Apply this paste directly onto the stain. Use your soft-bristle brush to gently work the paste into the fabric. Let it sit for about 10-15 minutes. Baking soda is a natural deodorizer and cleaner. It helps break down many types of stains. After it sits, gently scrub the area again.

Rinse the spot thoroughly with a damp cloth. Make sure all paste is gone. For oil-based stains, a small drop of dish soap directly on the stain can work. Dish soap is good at cutting grease. Apply it, let it sit for a few minutes, then gently scrub. Rinse thoroughly. Always test any strong cleaning agent in a small, hidden area first. This checks for color changes or damage. You want to avoid making the problem worse.

You might encounter more difficult stains. Ink or paint, for instance, can be very tough. For these, professional cleaning might be necessary. However, for most everyday stains, gentle spot treatment works. Be patient and repeat the process if needed. This careful approach helps your Hey Dudes look their best. It keeps them clean and neat.

## Proper Drying Techniques for Hand Washed Hey Dudes

After hand washing your Hey Dudes, proper drying is crucial. This step prevents mildew. It also helps your shoes keep their shape. Incorrect drying can lead to damage. It can also make your shoes smell bad. I always ensure they are fully dry before wearing them.

First, gently blot excess water from the shoes with a clean towel. Press the towel against the fabric. Do not twist or wring the shoes. This can stretch or deform them. You want to remove as much surface water as possible. This speeds up the drying process. It also reduces dripping.

Next, reshape your Hey Dudes. Stuff them with paper towels or a shoe tree. Paper towels absorb moisture from the inside. They also help the shoe hold its original form. Crumple the paper towels loosely. Do not pack them too tightly. This can stretch the shoe. Change the paper towels every few hours if they become very damp. This helps dry the inside faster. You can also use a cedar shoe tree. These absorb moisture naturally.

Place your Hey Dudes in a well-ventilated area. Choose a spot away from direct sunlight or heat sources. Direct sun can fade colors. High heat can shrink or crack materials. A fan can help air circulate. This speeds up drying. A porch, a sunny room, or near a window are good choices. Make sure there is good airflow around the shoes.

Drying Hey Dudes can take 12 to 24 hours. The exact time depends on humidity levels. It also depends on how wet the shoes got. Do not rush the drying process. Do not wear them until they are completely dry. Wearing damp shoes can cause odors. It can also damage the shoe structure. Patience here is key. This careful drying helps your Hey Dudes stay in good condition. It keeps them fresh and ready for wear. General shoe care practices apply here too. ## FAQ Section

### Can I machine wash Hey Dudes instead of hand washing?
Machine washing Hey Dudes is not recommended. The washing machine can be too rough. It might damage the fabric or alter the shoe's shape. High heat from a dryer can also cause shrinking. Hand washing is the safest method. It protects the shoe's integrity and colors.

### How often should I clean my Hey Dudes?
Clean your Hey Dudes as needed. If you wear them often, clean them every few weeks. If they get very dirty, clean them right away. Regular light cleaning prevents heavy dirt buildup. This makes deeper cleaning less frequent. It keeps them looking fresh.

### What kind of soap is best for hand washing Hey Dudes?
Use a mild laundry detergent or gentle dish soap. Look for clear, dye-free options. Harsh chemicals can damage the fabric or cause discoloration. A small amount goes a long way. Always dilute the soap with cool water before applying it.

### Can I use bleach on white Hey Dudes?
Avoid using bleach on your Hey Dudes, even white ones. Bleach can weaken the fabric fibers. It can also cause yellowing over time. For white Hey Dudes, stick to mild soap and water. Baking soda paste can help with tougher white stains.

### How long does it take for Hey Dudes to air dry?
Hey Dudes typically take 12 to 24 hours to air dry completely. Drying time depends on air circulation and humidity. Ensure they are fully dry before wearing them. Wearing damp shoes can cause odors or damage. Place them in a well-ventilated area away from direct heat.

### What should I do if my Hey Dudes still smell after cleaning?
If your Hey Dudes still smell, clean the insoles thoroughly. Insoles often hold most odors. You can also sprinkle baking soda inside the dry shoes. Let it sit overnight. Then, shake out the baking soda. Shoe deodorizers or cedar shoe trees also help absorb odors.
