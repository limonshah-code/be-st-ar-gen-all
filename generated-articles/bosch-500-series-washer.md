### Maintaining Your Bosch 500 Series Washer for Longevity

#### Cleaning the Detergent Dispenser

The detergent dispenser can accumulate residue over time. This can lead to clogs or inefficient dispensing. You should clean the dispenser regularly, perhaps once a month. Most dispensers are removable. Take it out and rinse it under warm water. Use a small brush to scrub away any sticky buildup.

If the dispenser is not removable, wipe it down with a damp cloth. Make sure to clean all compartments. This ensures your detergent and fabric softener dispense properly during each wash. A clean dispenser helps keep your clothes fresh.

#### Caring for the Door Seal

Front-load washers have a rubber gasket or door seal around the opening. This seal can trap moisture, lint, and detergent residue. Over time, this can lead to mold or mildew growth and unpleasant odors. After each wash, wipe down the door seal with a cloth. Leave the washer door ajar for a while to allow the drum and seal to air dry.

Periodically, you should clean the seal more thoroughly. Use a mixture of warm water and mild detergent or a dedicated washer cleaner. Wipe away any black spots or grime. This simple step is critical for preventing odors and maintaining a hygienic washer.
