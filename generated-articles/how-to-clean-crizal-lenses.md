## How to Clean Crizal Lenses Effectively

Do your Crizal lenses look cloudy or perpetually smudged? It feels frustrating when your vision is not crisp. Cleaning Crizal lenses correctly is not just about clear sight. It is about protecting a sophisticated piece of eyewear. These lenses have special anti-reflective coatings. These coatings reduce glare and repel dirt. Because of this, they need a specific cleaning approach. Using the wrong products or methods can harm this delicate coating. Such damage leads to haziness or scratches.
