## Step-by-Step Guide: How to Clean Your Dyson Airwrap Filter

Cleaning your Dyson Airwrap filter is a straightforward process. You can complete it in minutes. Follow these steps for effective filter maintenance. This regular cleaning keeps your device performing at its best. It helps you avoid common issues.

### Step 1: Disconnect and Prepare

Safety first. Always unplug your Dyson Airwrap from the power outlet. Make sure the device is completely off. Let it cool down if you just used it. The filter is at the bottom of the handle. It looks like a mesh cage. This cage protects the motor.

### Step 2: Remove the Filter Cage

Hold the main body of the Airwrap firmly. Grip the filter cage at the very bottom. Twist the filter cage anticlockwise. You will hear a click. This unlocks the cage. Now, gently pull the cage downwards. It should slide off easily. Be careful not to force it.

### Step 3: Clean the Filter Mesh

Take your Dyson filter cleaning brush. Slide it over the mesh part of the filter cage. The brush bristles are designed to fit the mesh. Move the brush up and down. Twist it around the filter cage. This action dislodges trapped dust, lint, and hair. You will see debris fall off. Continue brushing until the mesh looks clear. For stubborn grime, you can rinse the cage under cold water, but ensure it is completely dry before reattaching. If you are wondering how to clean your Dyson Airwrap filter without a brush, a soft, dry toothbrush can be a substitute, but the official tool is best.

### Step 4: Clean the Inner Mesh

Look inside the main Airwrap handle. You will see another mesh filter there. This is part of the filter assembly. Use the same cleaning brush. Gently slide it into this inner mesh. Rotate the brush to pick up any debris. You can also tap the handle gently. This helps dislodge loose particles. Do not push the brush too deep.

### Step 5: Wipe Down and Reassemble

Use your soft, dry cloth. Wipe the outside of the filter cage. Remove any dust or residue. Make sure both the outer cage and inner mesh are completely dry. If you used water, let the cage air dry for several hours. Once dry, align the filter cage with the handle. Push it up gently. Twist it clockwise until it clicks into place. This locks the filter cage securely.

Regularly performing these steps will maintain your Airwrap's power. It ensures efficient styling every time. I always follow these exact instructions. My Airwrap works like new after each clean. For more general Dyson filter care, you might find tips on how to clean a Dyson Air filter useful.

## FAQ Section

### How often should I clean my Dyson Airwrap filter?
You should clean your Dyson Airwrap filter regularly. If you use it daily, clean it every week or two. For less frequent use, check and clean it monthly. This proactive approach prevents performance issues. It helps avoid the white error light.

### What happens if I don't clean my Dyson Airwrap filter?
Not cleaning your Dyson Airwrap filter leads to reduced airflow. The motor will strain and may overheat. Your device might pulse, cut out, or display a white error light. Performance will drop, and the Airwrap's lifespan can shorten significantly.

### Can I use water to clean my Dyson Airwrap filter?
You can rinse the detachable filter cage under cold water if it is very dirty. However, it must be completely dry before reattaching. Never use water on the inner mesh inside the main handle. Use the dry cleaning brush for the inner part to avoid damage.

### What tools do I need to clean the filter?
You primarily need the Dyson Airwrap filter cleaning brush that came with your device. A soft, dry cloth is also helpful for wiping down the cage. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive materials. The provided brush fits perfectly and cleans effectively.

### My Dyson Airwrap is still pulsating after cleaning. What should I do?
If your Dyson Airwrap still pulsates, re-clean the filter thoroughly. Ensure both the outer cage and the inner mesh inside the handle are completely free of debris. Make sure the filter cage is correctly reattached and locked into place. Sometimes, a full power cycle (unplugging for 30 minutes) can also help reset it.

### Where is the filter on the Dyson Airwrap located?
The filter on the Dyson Airwrap is located at the very bottom of the handle. It is a cylindrical mesh cage that twists off. This cage covers the air intake and protects the motor from dust, lint, and hair product buildup.
