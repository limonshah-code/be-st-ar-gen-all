Stepping into a fresh pair of sandals on a warm day is a simple joy. Over time, however, our favorite open-toed footwear can lose its luster. The footbeds absorb sweat, dirt, and oils. This leads to discoloration, odors, and a less inviting appearance. Learning how to clean footbed of sandals properly is essential for hygiene and extending the life of your beloved shoes.
