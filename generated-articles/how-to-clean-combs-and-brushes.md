## Revitalize Your Hair Tools: How to Clean Combs and Brushes Effectively

#### Introduction
Do your hair tools look less than sparkling? Hair combs and brushes gather a surprising amount of grime. They collect hair, product residue, dust, and even bacteria. Using dirty tools on clean hair can defeat the purpose of washing your hair. It can also affect your hair's health and appearance. Understanding **how to clean combs and brushes** correctly is vital. This guide provides simple, effective methods. I will walk you through the essential steps. You will learn about different cleaning solutions. You will also discover tips for drying and maintaining various tool types. Keeping your tools clean helps your hair stay healthy. It also makes your combs and brushes last longer. A clean brush moves smoothly through your hair. It does not re-deposit old dirt. This leads to shinier, healthier hair over time. It is a small effort with big rewards for your beauty routine.
