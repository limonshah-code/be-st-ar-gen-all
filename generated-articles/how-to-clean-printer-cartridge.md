Are your printouts looking faded, streaky, or incomplete? Does your printer seem to be fighting you every time you hit 'print'? You are not alone. These common printer problems often come from dirty or clogged printer cartridges. Instead of buying a new cartridge, you can often bring your old one back to life. Learning how to clean printer cartridge can save you money and frustration. This guide will walk you through the simple steps.
