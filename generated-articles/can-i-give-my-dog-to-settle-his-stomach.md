## Safely Settle Your Dog's Upset Stomach: What You Can Give

It is tough to see your beloved dog feel unwell. A dog with an upset stomach often shows signs like vomiting, diarrhea, or a lack of appetite. As pet owners, we instinctively want to help our furry friends feel better fast. Many wonder, "Can I give my dog to settle his stomach using home remedies?" Yes, some safe options exist, but understanding when to act and when to seek veterinary help is vital. This article will guide you through recognizing symptoms. We will discuss safe home remedies, appropriate foods, and important medical considerations. You will learn the best ways to support your dog's recovery and prevent future digestive issues.

**Takeaway:**
*   Monitor your dog's symptoms closely for any worsening signs.
*   Offer bland, easy-to-digest foods like plain boiled chicken and rice.
*   Ensure constant access to fresh water to prevent dehydration.
*   Avoid fatty, spicy, or sugary human foods.
*   Consult your veterinarian immediately if symptoms are severe or persist.
