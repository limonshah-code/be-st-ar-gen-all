Have you ever looked closely at your hair brush? It collects more than just hair. Dirt, oils, product buildup, and even dead skin cells gather there. A dirty brush can transfer these things back to your clean hair. Cleaning your hair brushes regularly is important. It helps keep your hair clean and healthy.
